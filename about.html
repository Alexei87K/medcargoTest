About Us
Freight forwarding company “MedCargo” are specialized in cargo transportation via whole Europe and to the CIS countries.  Our offer, which based on fast and professional cargo collecting and further forwarding contains next:

  • Groupage and full cargo transportation via Europe (LT, EST, PL, DE, NL, SE, FI, FR, IT) and etc.
  • Freight forwarding to the CIS countries (Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan)

Moreover, full service of documents preparing, also custom, warehouse and brokerage services.


The Main Pluses of Our Company - speed, reliability, flexible pricing, laundry goods expedition.

With us you can be sure, that your goods will be delivered just in time.
